Privacy Policy

Ray App Lab (hereinafter referred to as "the Developer") establishes the following privacy policy regarding the handling of user information in the Android application "Quantiventra" (hereinafter referred to as "the App").

1. Collection and Use of Personal Information

The App does not collect or acquire any personal information such as the user's name, address, phone number, or email address. Furthermore, we do not transmit any acquired information to external servers or provide it to third parties.

2. Purpose of Using Device Permissions

The App uses the following permissions solely to provide its features, and will not use them for any other purposes.

  • Camera (CAMERA): Used exclusively for scanning barcodes and 2D codes. We do not record, save, or externally transmit any photos or videos.
  • Storage (READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Used exclusively for reading (importing) and writing (exporting) inventory data CSV files. We do not access any data other than the specified files.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data entered or scanned within the App is saved strictly on the user's device. The Developer does not have access to the user's data. The management of the data is the sole responsibility of the user.

4. Changes to the Privacy Policy

The Developer may revise this policy due to changes in laws and regulations or app updates. If there are significant changes, we will announce them on this page.

5. Contact Us

For inquiries regarding this policy, please contact us via the contact information on our website or the developer contact information provided on the app store.
