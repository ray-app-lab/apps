Section 01

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Focus Flame handles information when you use the app.

Focus Flame is a focus timer app. The app is designed to work mainly with local device data, while advertising and purchase features may use Google services.

✅
No Account Required Focus Flame does not require you to create an account. Your timer data and settings are stored locally on your device.
Section 02

Information Stored by the App

The app may store the following information on your device:

Data TypeExamples
Timer settingsFocus duration, short break duration, long break duration
PreferencesSelected focus mode, notification, sound, and vibration preferences
Theme selectionCurrently selected colour theme preset
Session history & statisticsCompleted session counts, total focus time, session log
Purchase stateWhether Remove Ads or Premium Themes has been purchased
ℹ️
All of the above data is stored locally on your device. Focus Flame does not operate a server to collect or sync this data.
Section 03

How Information Is Used

Information stored by the app is used solely to provide and improve the app's features:

PurposeDetails
Running the timerUsing your duration settings to count down sessions
Showing statisticsDisplaying session counts, focus time, and calendar streaks
Restoring settingsRemembering your preferences between app launches
Ad stateShowing or hiding ads based on whether Remove Ads has been purchased
Theme unlockingEnabling premium theme presets after purchase
Section 04

Third-Party Services

Focus Flame may use the following Google services:

ServicePurpose
Google AdMobDisplaying interstitial advertisements
Google Play BillingProcessing in-app purchases
⚠️
Google's Privacy Policies Apply These services may process data according to Google's own privacy policies and terms. Focus Flame does not control how Google processes data through these services. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
Section 05

Advertising

The app may show interstitial ads after completed focus sessions.

If you purchase Remove Ads, the app will stop showing those completion ads.

ℹ️
Before public release, the app uses Google test ad IDs. Production ad IDs must be configured before the app is published to the Play Store.
Section 06

Purchases

The app may offer one-time in-app purchases:

PurchaseWhat It Does
Remove AdsPermanently removes interstitial ads after completed sessions
Premium ThemesUnlocks 10 additional colour theme presets in Settings

Purchase processing is handled through Google Play Billing. The app stores purchase state locally so that purchased features are reflected in the user interface.

✅
One-Time Purchases Both Remove Ads and Premium Themes are one-time purchases. There are no subscriptions or recurring charges.
Section 07

Data Sharing

Focus Flame does not operate a custom server for user accounts or cloud sync. No session data, statistics, or personal information is sent to the developer's own servers.

However, Google services used for ads and billing may receive or process data as part of their own functionality. This is governed by Google's policies, not Focus Flame's.

Section 08

Data Retention & Deletion

Most app-generated data is stored locally on your device. You can remove it in either of the following ways:

MethodEffect
Clear app storage (via device Settings)Deletes all locally stored data including history and preferences
Uninstall the appRemoves the app and all associated local data
ℹ️
Purchase Records Purchase records are managed through Google Play and may remain available through your Google account according to Google's policies. This allows you to restore purchases if you reinstall the app.
Section 09

Children's Privacy

Focus Flame is not specifically directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

⚠️
If the app's target audience changes in a future release, this policy and Play Console audience settings should be reviewed before publication.
Section 10

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when app features, advertising, billing, or data handling changes. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised whenever changes are made.

Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
