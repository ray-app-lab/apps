Quantiventra

Optimize your inventory management with your smartphone or tablet.

A barcode scanning app designed for logistics professionals. No dedicated hardware required—ready to use on site.

Core Features

The following section outlines the key features provided by Quantiventra. Discover how each function can support your daily inventory management tasks.

📸 Camera Scan

Use your device's camera to read barcodes and 2D codes (including QR codes).

🔍 Item Search (Partial Match)

Even without a barcode, quickly narrow down items by entering just a part of the item name using the partial match search feature.

📍 Location Management

Record and manage storage locations (e.g., shelf numbers or areas) for each item, significantly boosting efficiency during picking and stock checks.

🧮 Smart Calc & Built-in Calculator

Directly enter calculation formulas (e.g., 10*5+3) in the quantity field, plus utilize a convenient built-in calculator. Powerfully supports tedious tallying tasks on site.

📄 Seamless CSV Integration

Easily import master data and export results via CSV. Fully compatible with Excel and PC-based workflows.

🔒 Offline & Privacy

100% offline processing. No data is sent to external servers. Your business data remains secure on your device.

Quantiventra Pro

Completely removes the functional limits of the free version. With a one-time purchase, you gain lifetime access to all advanced professional features.

  • Unlimited Data: Removes restrictions on master data registrations and history records.
  • Partner & Supplier Code Management: Link supplier or customer-specific part numbers to your internal IDs for seamless scanning and management.
  • Direct Stock Edit: Edit total inventory quantities directly on the spot.
  • Advanced CSV Options: Customize formats and access advanced export functionalities.
