Privacy Policy

Ray App Lab ("the Developer") establishes this privacy policy regarding the handling of user information in the Android application "Quantiventra" ("the Application").

1. Collection and Use of Personal Information

The Application does not collect or acquire any personal information, such as name, address, telephone number, or email address. We do not transmit any acquired information to external servers or provide it to third parties.

2. Purpose of Using Device Permissions

The Application uses the following permissions to realize its functions, but will not use them for any other purpose.

  • Camera (CAMERA): Used strictly for scanning barcodes and QR codes. We do not record, save, or transmit photos or videos to external sources.
  • Storage (READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Used strictly for importing and exporting inventory data in CSV file format. We do not access any data other than specified files.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data entered or scanned in the Application is stored locally on the user's device. The Developer cannot access your data. The management of data is the user's responsibility.

4. Changes to the Privacy Policy

The Developer may revise this policy in response to changes in laws or Application updates. Significant changes will be announced on this page.

5. Inquiries

For inquiries regarding this policy, please use the contact information provided on the Ray App Lab website or the developer contact information on the app store.
