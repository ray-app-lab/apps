Section 01

What Focus Flame Does

Focus Flame helps you run focused timer sessions. As time passes, the flame animation grows to show your progress — giving you a satisfying visual reward for staying on task.

▶ START

Begin a new focus session. The timer counts down and the flame begins to grow.

⏸ PAUSE

Temporarily stop the timer. The flame calms down while paused.

▶ RESUME

Continue a paused timer from where you left off.

↩ RESET

Stop the current timer and return to the default ready state.

Section 02

Home Screen

How to Start a Session

  1. Select a focus mode near the top of the screen.

  2. Choose a timer preset: 15, 25, 45, or 60 minutes.

  3. Tap START to begin your session.

Reading the Timer

ElementDescription
Large countdown displayShows remaining time in MM:SS format
Today counterNumber of completed sessions for the current day
Flame animationGrows as your session progresses; reacts to timer state

Section 07

If Purchase Buttons Do Nothing

Purchase buttons open Google Play Billing only when the billing connection and product setup are both ready. If nothing happens when you tap a purchase button, try the steps below.

  1. Check that Billing Status shows Ready in Settings. If it shows Connecting, wait a moment and try again.

  2. Make sure you have a stable internet connection.

  3. Close and reopen the app, then attempt the purchase again.

  4. If the issue persists, check that your Google Play account is active and in good standing.

⚠️
Play Console Configuration Required Billing depends on both the app and the Play Console configuration. If Play products are not configured, the purchase button may appear to do nothing even in a signed-in test environment.
ℹ️
Already Purchased? If Remove Ads or Premium Themes is not active, tap Restore Purchases in the Settings screen.
