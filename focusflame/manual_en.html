Focus Flame User Manual

A quick guide for using Focus Flame, a focus timer that grows a flame as your session progresses.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1. What Focus Flame Does

Focus Flame helps you run focused timer sessions. As time passes, the flame animation grows to show your progress.

  • START: begin a focus session
  • PAUSE: temporarily stop the timer
  • RESUME: continue a paused timer
  • RESET: stop the current timer and return to the default state

2. Home Screen

  • Select a focus mode near the top of the screen.
  • Choose a timer preset: 15, 25, 45, or 60 minutes.
  • Tap START to begin.
  • The large timer shows the remaining time in MM:SS format.
  • Today shows the number of completed sessions for the current day.

3. Settings Screen

  • Change focus, short break, and long break durations.
  • Turn completion notifications, sound, and vibration on or off.
  • Use Remove Ads to remove completion ads after purchase.
  • Use Premium Themes to unlock additional color themes after purchase.
  • Use Restore Purchases if a previous purchase is not reflected.

4. Flame and Themes

The flame reacts to the timer state.

  • Running: the flame grows with progress.
  • Paused: the flame becomes calmer.
  • Completed: a light sparkle or celebration effect appears.
  • Premium Themes: unlocks 10 selectable theme presets from Settings.

5. Stats Screen

  • View today, weekly, monthly, and all-time session counts.
  • Check total focus time.
  • View a mode breakdown bar chart.
  • Use the calendar to see which days had completed sessions.
  • Recent sessions are listed below the calendar.

6. Widget

Focus Flame includes a simple home screen widget.

  • The widget shows the remaining time.
  • It also shows the current mode and session progress.
  • Tap the widget to open the app.
The current widget is a minimal status widget. It does not include START, PAUSE, or RESET buttons yet.

7. If Purchase Buttons Do Nothing

Purchase buttons open Google Play Billing only when the billing connection and product setup are ready.

  • Check that Billing Status says Ready.
  • If it says Connecting, wait a moment and try again.
  • Play Console products must exist for real purchase testing.
  • Test purchases require a proper Google Play test environment.
Billing depends on both the app and the Play Console configuration. If Play products are not configured, the button may appear to do nothing.
