Privacy Policy

Ray App Lab (hereinafter referred to as "the Developer") establishes the following privacy policy regarding the handling of user information in the Android application "Quantiventra Stock" (hereinafter referred to as "the App").

1. Information Used and Purpose of Use

The App uses the following information for stock management, CSV import, shared-folder sync, and in-app purchase status confirmation.

  • Product and stock information: Product name, barcode, theoretical stock, current stock, and location are stored inside the App and used for inventory management features.
  • CSV files in the shared folder: Used for reading and writing CSV files for integration with Quantiventra.
  • In-app purchase ownership status: Used through Google Play Billing to confirm and restore ownership of Pro features.

2. Permissions and Access Used by the App

  • Access to a shared folder: Used only for reading and writing CSV files and marker files in the folder selected by the user.
  • Google Play Billing: Used only for confirming and restoring the one-time Pro purchase.

3. Where Data Is Stored

Product information, stock information, and app settings handled by the App are mainly stored on the user's device. If the user enables shared-folder sync, CSV files and marker files may also be stored in the selected shared folder.

4. Third-Party Sharing

The Developer does not send product or stock data handled by the App to the Developer's own servers, and does not sell or provide such data to third parties. However, Google Play services are used for in-app purchase confirmation.

5. Shared-Folder Integration

The App uses a shared-folder method to integrate with Quantiventra. The shared folder may contain files such as quantiventra_master.csv, inventory_review_data_yyyyMMdd_HHmm.csv, and marker files for each app. These files are handled only inside the folder selected by the user.

6. Changes to This Policy

The Developer may revise this policy when necessary. If important changes are made, they will be announced through the app store listing, distribution page, or related notices.

7. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, please use the contact information on Google Play or the developer contact listed in the app store.
