Focus Flame Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Focus Flame handles information when you use the app.

1. Overview

Focus Flame is a focus timer app. The app is designed to work mainly with local device data, while advertising and purchase features may use Google services.

Focus Flame does not require you to create an account.

2. Information Stored by the App

The app may store the following information on your device:

  • Timer settings, such as focus duration and break duration.
  • Selected focus mode.
  • Notification, sound, and vibration preferences.
  • Selected theme preference.
  • Session history and statistics generated inside the app.
  • Purchase state, such as Remove Ads or Premium Themes status.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used to provide and improve app features, including:

  • Running the focus timer.
  • Showing session statistics and streaks.
  • Restoring your settings.
  • Showing or hiding ads based on purchase state.
  • Unlocking premium themes after purchase.

4. Third-Party Services

Focus Flame may use the following Google services:

  • Google AdMob for advertising.
  • Google Play Billing for in-app purchases.

These services may process data according to Google's own privacy policies and terms. Focus Flame does not control how Google processes data through these services.

5. Advertising

The app may show interstitial ads after completed sessions. If you purchase Remove Ads, the app is intended to stop showing those ads.

Before release, the app uses Google test ad IDs. Production ad IDs must be configured before public release.

6. Purchases

The app may offer one-time purchases such as Remove Ads and Premium Themes. Purchase processing is handled through Google Play Billing.

The app stores purchase state locally so that purchased features can be reflected in the user interface.

7. Data Sharing

Focus Flame does not operate a custom server for user accounts or cloud sync. However, Google services used for ads and billing may receive or process data as part of their functionality.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Most app-generated data is stored locally on your device. You can remove locally stored app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

Purchase records are managed through Google Play and may remain available through your Google account according to Google's policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Focus Flame is not specifically directed to children. If release targeting changes, this policy and Play Console settings should be reviewed before publication.

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when app features, advertising, billing, or data handling changes. The updated date will be revised when changes are made.

11. Contact

Contact information must be added before public release.
